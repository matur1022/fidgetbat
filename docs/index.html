Why this works

Fidgeting isn't a bug.
It's how some brains focus.

If you've ever clicked a pen for an entire lecture or spun your phone through a whole meeting — this is for you. The research on "restless hands" is genuinely interesting:

🔬

Movement ↔ attention

UC Davis researchers found fidgeting was associated with better cognitive performance in people with ADHD — in kids and adults.

⏳

It grows over time

In a 2024 study of adults with ADHD, the longer a task ran, the more fidgeting helped — exactly when long videos start losing you.

🖐️

The second-task trick

Attention experts describe how a mindless secondary activity can help the brain stay locked on the primary one. FidgetBat is that secondary task — built into YouTube.

FidgetBat is a toy, not a treatment — we just think the research above is a great excuse to hit a ball with a bat while you watch videos. If focus struggles are getting in your way, a clinician beats a Chrome extension.

Designed for fidgeting

A toy, not a game you can lose

No timers. No fail states. No "game over". Just satisfying physics that's always there when your hands get restless — and quietly deep when you want it to be.

🏏

Real swing physics

The bat has weight and whip. Slow circles feel lazy; a hard flick launches the ball with hitstop, screen shake and a crack that scales with how hard you connected.

👻

Ghost mode

One click makes the whole toy ~75% transparent with clean outlines — you fidget without blocking the video. The point is watching, after all.

🎯

Skill that sneaks up on you

Only the barrel scores, the tip pays double, and spinning the bat like a blender earns nothing. You'll get better without noticing you were trying.

🔊

Every bat sounds different

Wood thocks. Metal pings. The lightsaber hums. A pool noodle... thuds. Swing sounds and hit sounds change with every bat you equip — or mute it all.

▶️

YouTube stays in charge

Clicks pass straight through to the video unless you're grabbing the bat. Pause, scrub, fullscreen — everything works. The game pauses itself in background tabs.

💾

Progress that's always yours

Hits, combos, unlocks and equipped gear save automatically per difficulty. Close the tab mid-swing; it's all there tomorrow.

Get it

Installed in about 60 seconds

1

Download the extension: ⬇ fidgetbat.zip  then unzip it anywhere you'll remember.

2

Open chrome://extensions in Chrome and flip on Developer mode (top-right toggle).

3

Click Load unpacked and pick the unzipped FidgetBat folder.

4

Open any YouTube video and click the FidgetBat icon in your toolbar. Grab the bat. You know what to do.
